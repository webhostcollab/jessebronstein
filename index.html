About

Jesse Bronstein is a New York-based cinematographer with over eight years of experience in narrative, commercial, and music video work. Originally from the East Village, Jesse began his career as an actor on Broadway before studying opera at Manhattan School of Music and ultimately pursuing filmmaking at Hunter College.

His work spans major brands including Nike, Adidas, Meta, The North Face, Uber, T-Mobile, and JP Morgan. His narrative work has screened at Clermont-Ferrand, Palm Springs, Raindance, and DokuFest, and received a Vimeo Staff Pick.
